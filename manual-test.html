Rambam LawSys manual test checklist

  1. Open index.html.
  2. Load the sample course.
  3. Evaluate readiness and confirm a NO-GO for the missing required lecture.
  4. Confirm the duplicate Hart entries appear as a review defect.
  5. Confirm the missing optional reading appears as a warning.
  6. Confirm the visible run manifest has an ID, timestamp, counts, coverage, defects, and readiness.
  7. Remove the missing required lecture and one duplicate entry.
  8. Evaluate again and confirm GO with one non-blocking optional warning.
  9. Export the manifest and confirm it is valid JSON matching the visible run.
